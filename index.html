Totaram Sanadhya

Girmitya to Liberator

The Forgotten Hero and his contributions to abolishment of the Indentured Labour System.

  • Born in Uttar Pradesh, India.

  • Arrived in Fiji onboard Jumna.

  • Sets up petition for political representation and education for Indians in Fiji.

  • Sent letter to Mahatma Gandhi requesting English-speaking lawyer sent to Fiji.

  • Published My twenty-One Years in the Fiji Islands (in Hindi) describing his experiences in Fiji.

  • Indentured labour system was abolished.

  • Joined Mahatma Gandhi at the Sabarmati Ashram.

  • Died from a prolonged illness after living a well-lived life.
