Totaram Sanadhya

Girmitya to Liberator

The Forgotten Hero and his contributions to abolishment of the Indentured Labour System.

Timeline

  • 1876 - Totaram born in Uttar Pradesh, India in 1876
  • 1893 - Arrived in Fiji onboard Jumna on 28th May
  • 1910 - Sets up petition for political representation and education for Indians in Fiji
  • 1912 - Sent letter to Mahatma Gandhi requesting English-speaking lawyer to be sent to Fiji, which saw the arrival of Manilal Maganlal Doctor and collectively worked towards obataining fairer life for labourers.
  • 1914 - Published My twenty-One Years in the Fiji Islands (in Hindi) describing his experiences in Fiji
  • 1917 - Indentured labour system was abolished.
  • 1922 - Joined Mahatma Gandhi at the Sabarmati Ashram
  • 1947 - Died from a prolonged illness after living a well-lived life.
